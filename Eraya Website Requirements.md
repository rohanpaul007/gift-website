Eraya Website Requirements

Project Overview

I want a clean, modern, premium, and mobile-friendly one-page website for my brand Eraya. The website should feel minimal, elegant, and trustworthy while reflecting our mission of redefining the meaning of customized gifting.

The primary goal of the website is to generate enquiries, not online purchases. Customers should be able to explore our products and contact us through WhatsApp, email, or the enquiry form.

Overall Goal

The website should not feel like a typical printing or gifting website.

It should communicate that Eraya is a brand built on thoughtful, meaningful gifting—where every product is designed to strengthen relationships, celebrate milestones, and create lasting memories.

The overall experience should feel premium, trustworthy, emotional, and founder-led, helping visitors connect with the story behind the brand while making it easy for them to enquire about customized products.
