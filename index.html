Meet the Founder

Hi, I'm Mahek Shaw.

Founder of Eraya.

I've always believed that the most meaningful gifts aren't the most expensive — they're the ones that make someone feel seen, appreciated, and remembered. That's why I'm building Eraya: to redefine the way people think about customized gifting.

For me, customization isn't simply about adding a logo or printing a name. It's about creating something that reflects your story, your values, and the relationships you want to strengthen. Whether it's a business celebrating its employees or someone looking for the perfect personalized gift, I want every creation from Eraya to leave a lasting impression.

Thank you for being part of this journey. Let's create something meaningful together.
